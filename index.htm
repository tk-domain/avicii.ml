As i'm huge fan of Electronic dance music from ambient to hardcore and house to drum n bass i'm deeply shocked about his death.to be honest,i wasn't a big fan of this kind of music,very cheesy,not so complicated,catchy edm pop or whatever you may call it,but one thing always put Tim on the highest place in my personal EDM ranking than Hardwell, Kshmr, David Guetta, W&W, new Armin van Buuren, Timmy Trumpet ,Selloutiësto or Steve Aoki. This thing is - passion. Yes,big passion to one and only one genre of music ,poppy,cheesy ,full of vocal pop-dance music -his music,straight from his heart. I think Tim's passion to his music ,especially now,in 2018 ,when we are the witness of totally garbage in EDM (Steve Aoki producing (?) psytrance (??),or Hardwell goes Hardstyle) ,is something very unique. Everything in edm without guys like Avicii gone totally in wrong place. We expecting new collaborations,new sounds ,new styles a of music .. we're not expecting QuALITY and PASSION in music. Good question is what will be next,i guess maybe second chance to gabber or bubblegum pop with black mc's like in 90s.Big Shame to edm producers .rest in peace tim
